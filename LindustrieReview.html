L'industrie: A review of one of my favoutites

In a city flooded with TikTok food trends, it’s easy to become skeptical about the hype. But then there’s L’Industrie Pizza—a tiny slice shop in Williamsburg, Brooklyn, that not only lives up to its TikTok fame but exceeds every expectation. It's one of those rare spots where the social media buzz actually translates to something spectacular in real life.

L'Industrie offers a perfect fusion of classic New York-style pizza and an innovative, modern twist. While the place is small and unassuming, the pizza is nothing short of extraordinary. The crust is what truly stands out—thin, with a delightful crunch, yet still maintaining the chewy texture that makes for the perfect bite. It’s that quintessential New York slice, but taken up a notch with incredibly fresh ingredients.

I decided to try their signature Burrata Slice, and it was beyond anything I'd anticipated. The creamy burrata oozing on top of the hot, crispy pizza base was an indulgent combination that somehow felt both luxurious and unpretentious. The sauce is a classic, tangy tomato base with the perfect amount of acidity, balanced by a hint of sweetness. It’s not overpowering but acts as a delicious canvas that enhances the flavors of the fresh ingredients layered on top. The burrata is what makes this slice exceptional. Soft and creamy, it’s generously placed on the pizza after it’s been baked, allowing the heat of the crust to gently warm the cheese, creating a luscious, gooey texture that contrasts beautifully with the crisp crust beneath.

And then comes the olive oil—drizzled with a light hand, it adds just the right amount of richness and a hint of fruitiness without making the slice too greasy. The oil mingles with the burrata, creating pockets of flavor that burst as you bite into the slice. A sprinkle of fresh basil often accompanies the burrata, adding a fragrant, herbal note that elevates the entire experience. Every element is balanced perfectly, making the slice indulgent yet somehow light and easy to devour.

Now, while their pizza takes the spotlight, L'Industrie's Tiramisu is not to be overlooked. Served in a small cup, it is the perfect complement to the savory experience. The dessert is delightfully creamy, with layers of rich mascarpone cheese and espresso-soaked ladyfingers that melt in your mouth. It strikes a perfect balance between sweet and bitter, with the coffee flavor providing depth without being overwhelming. The cocoa dusting on top adds a slight bitterness that enhances the sweetness of the mascarpone, making it a dessert that leaves you wanting just one more spoonful.

What sets L’Industrie apart from other trendy spots is the genuine care they put into each slice. It’s easy to see why it’s gone viral, but it’s the consistent quality, the thoughtful combinations, and the laid-back vibe that make it a place you’ll want to return to over and over again.

Despite the lines and the TikTok-induced rush, the wait is always worth it. The friendly staff manages to keep things moving while maintaining the integrity of every pizza they serve. Whether you’re here for a quick slice or a leisurely pizza adventure, L’Industrie has a way of making the experience feel special.

In a world where many social media-hyped places fall flat, L’Industrie Pizza stands tall, delivering one of the best slices in New York City. It’s not just a TikTok sensation—it’s a pizza lover’s dream come true.
