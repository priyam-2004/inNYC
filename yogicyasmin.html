Yogic Yasmin

Project Overview - Jan - Feb, 2024 / Typeface Design

Software used - Adobe illustrator, Adobe photoshop

Challenge - To design a typefcae based on an interview with a strager

Solution - Create a yoga inspired typefcae rooting from "Yasmin's" love for spirituality and yoga 

More Info

In the rhythm of letters, my typeface design embodies the essence of yoga. Each character morphs into an abstract figure engaged in various yoga poses and stretches, embracing the diversity of body types. This design is a testament to the inclusivity of the ancient practice.
